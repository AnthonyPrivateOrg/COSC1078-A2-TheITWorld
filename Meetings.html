11/05/2021 Meeting (Face-to-Face)

Agenda:

This meeting was our first meeting after assignment 2, however, we had decided not to assignment sections to members. The reasons for this is, we found it would be more beneficial for each member to read through the assignment specification in their own time and pick a few sections they wish to work on. Although we didn’t assignment sections, we discussed what our assignment artefacts would be, and decided that we would develop a mobile app prototype with either Figma or Proto.io, as well as develop a miniature of the project idea using an Arduino. The final topic on the agenda was to confirm that everyone was still fine with the regular meeting times of Wednesday, as it was important that we meet at least once online to discuss the assignment and make sure everyone is on track.

Actions:

Each member was tasked to read through the assignment specifications in their own time and pick a few sections they wish to work on. If they don’t do this, or are fine with working on any section, they will automatically be assigned the leftover sections. Each member is also to ensure that they can attend the Wednesday night meeting, if not they are to inform the group ahead of time, in which case they will automatically be assigned sections.

12/05/2021 Meeting (Online)

Agenda:

This meeting was the first official meeting regarding assignment 3. Although we had discussed a few things, mainly about the artefacts we could complete, earlier on, they were not conducted in proper meetings. The agenda for this meeting was assigning work to each member, going through the specifications and what we as a group expect of each other when completing our assigned sections.

When discussing deadlines, it was decided that the first deadline would be at the end of this week. Although we don’t expect a completed section, each member is expected to have at least written down dot points of what they plan to write for all their sections. They are also expected to upload there documents to both the GitHub and the MS Teams Files as proof.

Some other topics to discuss was how to improve from the last assignment, such as sticking to deadlines and looking over each other’s work more frequently. Everyone was also to update the ongoing timeframe document, as the timeframe section is to be done individually.

Most members were able to attend, however George had already informed us that he would not be able to attend. Although he did not attend, he had already informed the group that he would not be able to attend the meeting and had already messaged the team on which sections he wishes to work on.

Actions:

Each member is to start working on all their sections and show some form of proof that they have been working on it. They will also need to update the ongoing document, so that when the time comes to work on the timeframe section there is no confusion. Each member is also to make sure that they meet the deadlines set during the meeting. To check that deadlines are met, members are to ensure that they update the MS Teams files with their updated work or show some proof in terms of the artefacts.

18/05/2021 Meeting (Face-to-Face)

Agenda:

This meeting was the second face-to-face meeting where the group focused on assignment 3. In this meeting we ensured that George was caught up with everything that was mentioned in the previous meeting, and also checked that everyone had met the first deadline. The first deadline was for everyone to have posted a document with dot points for each of their sections, detailing what they plan to write. Although this deadline was relatively simple, it was not met by everyone, making it hard to know whether everyone was working on their sections or not. George was exempt from this, as he had missed the previous meeting and was working on the Arduino artefact instead, and had shown proof of his progress.

Artem was also missing during this meeting, due to work commitments. This was not an issue as his assigned sections were mostly individual. Victor and Anthony had also discussed on which tool to use to create the mobile app prototype, and had decided to use Figma.

Actions:

Everyone is to ensure that they meet the next deadline, which is to submit a rough draft of their sections by the end of the week, they will be reminded in tomorrow’s meeting. Anthony and Victor will also start working on the mobile app prototype, and should have a rough design by the end of the week. As the first deadline set was not met, it is important that the following one is met and that everyone provide some form of evidence that they did the work they said they would, as this was an issue with the previous assignment.

19/05/2021 Meeting (Online)

Agenda:

This meeting was our regularly scheduled online meeting, where we mainly focus on catching up on each other’s progress. We start off by going “around”, member to member, and each state what we have been working on, what we plan to do after the meeting until the next meeting, and, most importantly, any issues we encountered when working on the assignment. One other topic was discussing deadlines and whether or not we have met the previous deadline or not.

Starting with the first topic on the agenda, each member seemed to be making decent progress on the assignment, with minimal issues. However, the only issue was that some people weren’t posting into the MS Teams files section, to show they’re progress. This is important, as in the previous assignment some members were saying they were done but didn’t post their work until later.

Regarding the deadlines topic, it was noted that the team had not met the first agreed deadline. The first deadline, although not as important as the rest, was to post a document of dot points (or drafts) of each of their sections. This both help progress the assignment faster, as well as give each member a chance to do a quick review of each other’s work. As this deadline was not met, it was noted that the next deadline was very important and needed to be met, which was to post a draft of all their sections to the MS Teams and hopefully the GitHub if they have the chance.

Actions:

Everyone was to start writing up drafts of all their assigned sections and post whatever they have done by the next meeting (i.e. the face-to-face meeting on Tuesday). Sahil and George would also need to ensure that they post their progress so far to the MS Teams, so the team can look over them if they have the chance. Everyone also needed to make sure that they meet the next deadline, as it is a very important deadline.

A final note, was that everyone would need to come early to the Tuesday class (1:30pm), so that the team can work on the assignment together for a period of time before the scheduled class.

25/05/2021 Meeting (Face-to-Face)

Agenda:

This meeting is our final face-to-face meeting as it is now week 12 of the semester. The topics on the agenda for this meeting was discussing if we were on track or not, when to put everything together in both the report and the website, and any other issues. An important topic for this meeting was also discussing an unexpected event that occurred in the life of one of our members, and how we should handle it.

For the majority of the group, we had completed a draft of each of our assigned sections and would review them in the online meeting. However, part of the group had yet to submit any notable work, which worried the rest of the group about whether or not they would be able to complete the report on time.

Artem had unfortunately experienced a tragic event in his family, and the rest of the group decided that we were willing to take over some of his work. As such, we had discussed with our tutor about organising an extension, either for the group or just for Artem, to give Artem some personal time, and to give the group some time to pick up from where Artem had left off.

Finally, the majority of the class time would be used to work on the assignment, particularly the mobile app and finishing off the report sections.

Actions:

Everyone was to submit their work to the MS Teams for the rest of the team to review in the next online meeting. It is important that every member (with the exception of Artem) show a substantial amount of work as this would have been the final week to work on the assignment if the group was not granted an extension.

26/05/2021 Meeting (Online)

Agenda:

This meeting would have been our final regular scheduled online meeting for the assignment (if we weren’t given an extension), so the group treated it as such. We, as a group, looked through each member’s assigned section, reviewing their work and making notes on any changes that could be made to make our work better.

Most members had completed their drafts and only needed to make a few minor adjustments, which indicated that they had met the final deadline properly. However, it was noted that Shail had yet to show any substantial work, only showing either dot points or very short paragraphs. As a group, we had make it clear that he would need to pick up the pace and complete it work by our next meeting.

The group had also decided to schedule a meeting on Saturday, to hopefully finalise the group work by putting it together into a report and onto the website.

Actions:

Everyone should make the edits, which were noted by other members, to our own work and be ready to add them to the report and the website. Sahil would need to complete his sections, and the group would need to look over his work in the following meeting.

Artem, had decided that he could still work on some of his assigned sections, but would need some help in the “Plans and Progress” section, which the group has decided that everyone should contribute to.

As a group, everyone would also need to think about what to include in the group reflection and be ready to share their thoughts.
