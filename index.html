Group Processes

The group overall did quite decent. The group thought that discussing the specifications of the assignment and assigning work at the beginning was something that went really well. As each member was assigned their own individual parts, everyone was responsible for their own sections, which helped everyone focus on their own parts. Setting deadlines also helped the team to stay on track most of the time. This strategy would help make sure work was done on time instead of at the last minute. We were also relatively consistent with team meetings as well with the odd missing person here and there.

In assignment 3 it might be best to improve the quality of work compared to the previous assignment as it didn’t meet our expectations. Discussing our ideal standard of work at the beginning of the assignment would help with this, so that all members know what to aim for. The deadlines made in the previous assignment were met in the first half however people began to slack off so making sure we don’t overestimate this assignment will help towards this. The deadlines should also be set at the beginning so in the case of issues with work we will most likely have more time to review and edit work. Overall, following through with everything and making sure our processes are smooth will lessen stress in parts of this assignment if there are troubles.
