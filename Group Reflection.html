Group Reflection

Group's Reflection:

After working on the assignment, the group has come up with some aspects that we, as a group, believe went well. One aspect is that the progress with the prototype went well. We thought this went fairly well considering the limited time we were provided, as well as us trying to incorporate new ideas or improve previous ideas relating to the prototype and the miniature. As we had mad some decent progress, we had unexpectedly has time to improve the miniature and add new features. Another aspect, was that compared to our previous assignment (i.e. Assignment 2), the group was more on track for the most part. This relates to the group discussing the assignment specifications together and in more depth at the beginning of the assignment, which lead to the group having a good start. In terms of designing the report, the process was done slightly better and therefore was more efficient as compared to our last assignment.

In terms of what could be improved, the group had agreed that meeting deadlines is something that needs to be improved greatly. Just like the previous assignment, only two to three members had consistently met deadlines, meaning that the assignment work was delayed a lot of the time. Another aspect that could be improved is how the group controls the quality of the assignment. Although we had discussed the standard that the group wished to achieve, we had not reviewed each other’s work until the third week of working on the assignment, as we trusted that each member would meet this standard.

What the group found surprising, was that although we had trouble controlling the quality, the work that was completed was of decent quality. The level of depth that the group went into was also improved from the previous assignment and from our initial plan. The group was also surprised that the miniature turned out so well and that we were able to improve our initial idea so well.

Something that we learnt about groups is that it is difficult to manage everyone’s time in terms of finding a section of time where all members can meet. The group had occasions where it was difficult scheduling additional meetings, as everyone had different commitments.

In terms of our GitHub log, we believe that, excluding assignment 2’s log, then the git log would display our group work and distribution very well, once everyone uploads their work to the website.

Anthony's Group Reflection:

In terms of what went well, I believe that the group had started off well, going through the assignment specifications together, assigning sections, and discussing what we as a group expect of each other. This helped people know what they needed to do, and also reduced the amount of time spent editing work as the work would usually be up to standard.

There were a lot of things that could be improved, one being submitting work on time. This was not in terms of submitting the assignment on time, but each member submitting their work to the shared folder on time, to meet set deadlines. Almost half the members struggled to meet deadlines, making it difficult to stay on track with the report.

What surprised me was that the prototyping and the miniature turned out rather well. Initially it seemed like a lot of work in addition to the report, but the group did well in making a lot of progress on the artefacts early on. One other thing that surprised me, was that despite not meeting deadlines in the previous assignment, some members still didn’t meet deadlines and failed to inform the rest of the group.

What I learnt about groups is that it is difficult to make sure everyone is on track with the work. This was because this relies on everyone sharing their work, and sharing their struggles. But if people don’t share, it is difficult for the rest of the group to sympathise and help.

In my opinion, the git logs should display the group distribution better, as compared to the previous assignment. This is because we have already started off with a base, instead of having one person post the entire base, skewing the results.


George's Group Reflection:

I believe that compared to the last assignment, we did a lot better in making sure that we would stick to our deadlines and at least have drafts ready at a certain point in time where then we would have plenty of time to look over and edit. Starting on our website and report earlier was definitely a good idea as last assignment we left our report to the last minute and some of us had to rush to get the finer details done.

We did however encounter a terrible issue and that was the fact one of our team members went through a family tragedy and that really set us back as they were also struggling with their sections. Fortunately, we were given an extension, but it was still a bit rough with bringing quality up to the table for some sections. It was surprising however that we had built quite a decent miniature that looked good and worked as intended.

We originally thought that we wouldn’t have been able to push out this kind of quality, but I believe we did it quite well and were continuously thinking of ways to automate and improve systems.

I learned that it can be quite tough to have a team member not pushing out their best work because of certain issues as this can take up valuable time from other team members if they wished to take up that person’s parts and improve them.

Sahil's Group Reflection:

The things that went well were that everyone was getting their work done and not only that the artefact went well as we managed to make a protype with function LED and bollards. Also, the prototype design of the app that we made using Figma looked good and functioned the way we wanted it to function.

The things that could be improved better is having better communications and that everyone meets the groups deadlines on time as we got behind on making the report.

The thing that was surprising to me was that we managed to get so much done with the 3 weeks we had and how everyone was on top of things not only that I also found it surprising how much work we had done as well, and we didn’t aim for the bare minimum.

I learned about groups is that it is hard to get everyone together in one place at a certain time as people have things going on and makes is hard to meet up. Also depending on your group there is less stress involved as you do not have to be the one doing all the work like in high school and that everyone pulls their own weight.
