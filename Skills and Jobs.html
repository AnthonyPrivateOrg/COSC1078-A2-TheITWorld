Skills and Jobs

For this venture to be successful and to take our project to its furthest extent, Pie9 would like to see the amalgamation of the following team:

Marketing Director:

Pie9 desires to reduce the efforts of finding parking and through the use of our system, we believe we can accomplish this. To make certain our system will be heard and used by various users, Pie9’s team will need the induction of a marketing director to successfully navigate our marketing ventures and rally customers for our application.

As a marketing director, you will be tasked with the forefront of the ‘brand’ and its image and marketing/communication strategies that will be implemented within the organisation. You will oversee and provide guidance to the team in the marketing efforts undertaken. This will be accomplished by your analysis and evaluation of strategies and further, your development of strategies to implement. You will need to ensure the team is communicated and alerted of such adjustments to guarantee a successful marketing endeavour. Which communication approaches instilled in the team will also be managed by you as communication is vital in any team project. Additionally, you will work closely to the sales sector to devise a marketing budget and pricing strategy to maximise the profits generated by the organisation. Consequently, this will be achieved through the analysis of the market and the competitors present for our project.

This role in the organisation will entail you to be able to perform the following tasks:

  • Plan, develop, implement, execute and evaluate new and existing marketing strategies for the product/service.
  • Oversee and guide the team in its marketing efforts
  • Ensure the team is up to date with the marketing plan
  • Analyse our competitors and the trends
  • Analyse and determine potential customers
  • Set and administer the annual marketing budget for our product/service
  • Create and develop a brand strategy
  • Organise and sanction the organisation’s meetings/conferences
  • Organise and manage the promotional endeavours (promotional material, sales and leads generation, digital/social media promotions, etc.)

Furthermore, to ensure your tasks are handled and executed with finesse, as a marketing director you will require the following key skills:

  • Strong leadership and communication – Being able to deliver and express information to the team effectively is imperative. This needs to be supported with a strong authority and influence behind it to be able to successfully complete the tasks
  • Project and time management – It is necessary for a marketing director to be able to set deadlines, organise roles, ensure everyone is up to date and communication is always high. You need to be able to monitor and guide the daily operations of the team.
  • Data analysis – Staying on top of the trends and competitors in the market is crucial to guaranteeing the project will be a successful endeavour
  • Solution-Oriented – Dire in any position, the marketing director needs to be able to take on feedback and think ‘outside the box’. Taking an innovative and staying opened-minded will keep the team on top of all other competitors and ensure we are delivering a fresh and new experience to the customers.

As the marketing director for Pie9, you will surely be paving the way for an innovative and solution-oriented mindset for future generations. Pie9’s marketing director will lead the charge for the team and the venture we are proposing.

UX/UI Designer:

Our Story:

Pie9 is a company with the vision that parking your car should be easy and convenient, not something to worry and struggle with. We, as a team, have developed a project plan and prototype which has to potential to completely change the way humans find parking and are looking for a UX/UI Designer to join our small yet growing team and help us on making our idea a reality. You will be responsible for improving and developing a user friendly mobile interface for our application, and potentially a web application in the future. We hope that you will grow with us and will be given a level of autonomy to leave your own mark on our project.

We would also love to see your style of design and any previous projects you’ve worked on, so it would be great to provide some examples of your previous work or a portfolio when applying for this position.

Preferred Skills and Experiences:

  • The ability to develop new and exciting designs to appeal to our users
  • Experience in using prototyping tools
  • Ability to build-upon and maintain Figma prototypes
  • Good collaboration skills, working with different development teams

Work you will do:

  • Developing designs and turning complex functional requirements into user friendly and appealing designs
  • Maintaining and building on current interface designs
  • Writing documentation for designs to guide other development teams
  • Designing and developing Pie9’s mobile and web application

Why join our Team:

Although we are a small company, we are rapidly growing and already have plans for future development. You will have autonomy and any work you work on will be under your ownership. We are up to negotiate you salary, and will reward you for your best work. We also offer training and development funds to invest in you and hope to further develop your skills while working at Pie9.

Pie9 was formed under the belief that easy and convenient parking shouldn’t be a privilege – it’s a necessity and a right that every driver has. Our team has developed a prototype which has the potential to completely change the way humans look for parking and we are now looking for an engineer that will help us bring said prototype to life. Join our small team of great minds and help us reshape the future of parking.

Your role will be to turn our prototype into a finished product. We have a working system that needs to be integrated into a single board in a chassis of our design. So, if you believe yourself to be a specialist in electronic circuit design and are ready to work in a fast paced fast environment – we want you! Apply now and prepare to change the future of parking.

Preferred Skills and Experience:

  • Master each stage of the circuit board design
  • Good collaboration skills to work in a team
  • Responsibilities
  • Integrating an Arduino prototype into an all-in-one solution
  • Designing a PCB layout for developers to recreate on mass scale
